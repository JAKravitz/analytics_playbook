## Spectral uplift
- **MSI baseline:** Coarse driver classes from indices and change timing.
- **VNIR HSI adds:** Spectral separation of logging, fire, agricultural encroachment, and drought-induced decline.
- **VNIR-SWIR HSI adds:** Adds separation of drought stress vs. pathogen-induced decline via SWIR water and structural signals.

## Purpose and scope
Surface anomalies or ranked explanatory hypotheses from spectral and contextual data; not ground-truth cause without corroboration.
