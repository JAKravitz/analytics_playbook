## Spectral uplift
- **MSI baseline:** Limited MSI capability — full product requires VNIR HSI.
- **VNIR HSI adds:** Bleaching detected via loss of zooxanthellae pigment signal (550–600nm) — spectrally specific, not possible with MSI
- **VNIR-SWIR HSI adds:** No uplift — SWIR attenuates in water column
